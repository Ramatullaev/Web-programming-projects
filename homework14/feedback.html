Course Information

Course: How to drive F1

Instructor: Dr. Max Verstappen

This course takes students into the world of Formula 1 driving, covering racecraft, overtaking strategy, precision braking, and handling extreme speeds. Guided by a world-class instructor, you will learn how F1 drivers manage tires, fuel, and pressure during real competitive conditions.

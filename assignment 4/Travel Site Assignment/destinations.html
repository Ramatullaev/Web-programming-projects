Destinations

A quick starter list. Click a card title to jump to its anchor by ID.

Istanbul, Türkiye

Visit Hagia Sophia and the Grand Bazaar. Ride the ferry at sunset.

  • Local dish: balık ekmek
  • Best area: Kadıköy/Moda

Almaty, Kazakhstan

Shymbulak, Medeu, and breathtaking mountains just outside the city.

  • Local dish: Plov, beshbarmak
  • Best season: September–October

Dubai, UAE

Skyscrapers, beaches, desert safari. Consider a day trip to Abu Dhabi.

  • Transit: Metro Red Line
  • Season: Nov–Mar
